My Research

AI-POWERED RESEARCH COMPANION USING COHEREAI, MULTER AND PDF-PARSE

This project is designed to assist medical researchers and professionals by automatically extracting insights from research papers and clinical documents. Leveraging CohereAI’s natural language processing, PDF-Parse for document extraction, and Multer for secure file uploads, the system parses academic literature to identify critical patterns related to disease diagnosis and treatment strategies. Integrated with machine learning algorithms, it provides intelligent summarization, entity recognition, and relevance scoring — enabling faster, data-driven healthcare research and decision-making.
